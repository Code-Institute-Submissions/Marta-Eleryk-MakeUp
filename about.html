Marta Eleryk

I have been applying makeup for many years, but three years ago I changed career to follow my dreams as a professional makeup artist.

I am based on ​Columbia Road, ​ in​ Shoreditch ​ , East London so will happily travel within London or outside if required. I graduated from ​London Hair and Makeup School ​ studying​ ​ styles such as glamour, mature, dark skin makeup, as well as professional hair styling (hair on request).

My experience covers being part of commercial and fashion photoshoots, music videos, fashion shows and even assisting for artists like ​Lan Nguyen-Grealis ​ in a team​ ​ at the ​London Fashion Week ​ . I am also experienced in bridal and special occasion makeup.

I am passionate about achieving a natural makeup look; a clean and beautiful skin finish; and smooth product application, with an exceptional eye for detail. My core expertise is to enhance my client's natural beauty, using classic styles.

Over the years I have attended numerous masterclasses from the biggest makeup brands like ​MAC, Bobbi Brown, Urban Decay, Charlotte Tillbury, Cover FX ​ and​ Hourglass ​ to learn more about their products and techniques. Consequently, in my kit you will find these ranges, along with other high-end collections such as ​Armani, Lancome, Estee Lauder ​ and​ Chanel. ​ My client's receive the highest quality products, for the highest quality finish

I love being creative in every aspect of my life. I am therefore constantly evolving my techniques, learning new methods and have an open mind for fresh approaches. I always balance this creativity with the desires and wishes of my clients, so everyone goes home with a smile on their face.
